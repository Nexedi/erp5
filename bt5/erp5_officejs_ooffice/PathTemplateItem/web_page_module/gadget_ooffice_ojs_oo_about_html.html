OfficeJS Only Office Editor

OfficeJS Only Office editors are a progressive web application developed with renderJS and jIO, and based on OnlyOffice project. It is developed under the premises of privacy and availability meaning users are free to choose their storage, can work offline and synchronize their work easily. Only Office applications are developed, maintained and used by Nexedi, and their code can be found here.
